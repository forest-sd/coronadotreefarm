About Us

Coronado Tree Farm has been providing quality trees and expert advice to homeowners, landscapers, and municipalities for years. Our passion for trees drives everything we do, from careful cultivation to personalized customer service.

Our Story

What started as a small family operation has grown into a premier tree nursery serving the region. We take pride in growing healthy, well-rooted trees that thrive in local conditions and provide years of beauty and shade.

Our Expertise

Our team includes certified arborists and horticulturists who can help you select the right tree for your specific site conditions, soil type, and aesthetic preferences. We believe the right tree in the right place creates lasting value for your property and the environment.

Sustainability

We practice sustainable growing methods including water-efficient irrigation, integrated pest management, and soil health programs. Every tree we sell is grown with care for both quality and environmental responsibility.
