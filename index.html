Welcome

Coronado Tree Farm is a haven for nature enthusiasts and gardeners alike, nestled in a picturesque landscape that boasts a wide variety of trees. This unique farm offers more than just a collection of trees; it presents an opportunity to connect with nature and learn about sustainable tree care.

Whether you are looking for the perfect shade tree, a privacy screen of evergreens, or productive fruit trees for your backyard orchard, our knowledgeable staff is here to help you make the best choice for your landscape and climate.
