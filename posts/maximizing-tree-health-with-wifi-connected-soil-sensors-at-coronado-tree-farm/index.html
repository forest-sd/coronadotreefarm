Maximizing Tree Health with WiFi Connected Soil Sensors

Published: 2024 • By Coronado Tree Farm

Modern technology is revolutionizing tree care, and WiFi-connected soil sensors represent one of the most impactful innovations for nurseries and homeowners alike.

What Are Soil Sensors?

These compact devices measure soil moisture, temperature, and nutrient levels in real time, transmitting data to your smartphone or computer. This information enables precise, data-driven decisions about watering and fertilizing.

Benefits for Tree Health

  • Prevent both overwatering and underwatering
  • Optimize fertilizer application timing and rates
  • Early detection of drainage problems
  • Monitor root zone conditions across seasons

Water Conservation

By watering only when sensors indicate the soil has dried to an appropriate level, water use can be reduced by 30-50% compared to timer-based irrigation. This is especially valuable in drought-prone regions like Southern California.

At Coronado Tree Farm

We use soil sensor technology throughout our growing fields to ensure every tree receives optimal care. This results in healthier root systems and better-established trees that thrive after transplanting to your property.
