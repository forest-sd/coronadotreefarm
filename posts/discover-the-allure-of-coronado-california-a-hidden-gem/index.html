Discover the Allure of Coronado, California

Published: 2024 • By Coronado Tree Farm

Coronado, California is a stunning resort city located across the bay from downtown San Diego. Known for its beautiful beaches, historic architecture, and small-town charm, Coronado offers a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural richness.

Coronado Beach

Consistently ranked among America's best beaches, Coronado Beach stretches for 1.5 miles of wide, flat golden sand. The historic Hotel del Coronado, a National Historic Landmark built in 1888, provides a stunning backdrop.

Things to Do

  • Bike or walk along the Silver Strand
  • Visit the Coronado Museum of History and Art
  • Explore the Ferry Landing Marketplace
  • Enjoy water sports including kayaking and paddleboarding
  • Dine at excellent restaurants along Orange Avenue

Climate

Coronado enjoys a Mediterranean climate with mild temperatures year-round, making it an ideal location for outdoor activities and, of course, growing beautiful trees.
