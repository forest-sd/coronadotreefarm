Essential Practices in Sustainable Forestry and Tree Farming

Published: 2025 • By Coronado Tree Farm

Sustainable forestry ensures that forests and tree farms continue to provide ecological, economic, and social benefits for current and future generations.

Key Principles

  • Maintaining biodiversity within managed forests
  • Protecting water quality and watershed health
  • Preserving soil health through careful harvesting practices
  • Managing wildlife habitat alongside timber production

Sustainable Tree Farm Practices

At Coronado Tree Farm, we implement drip irrigation to conserve water, use integrated pest management to minimize chemical inputs, maintain diverse plantings to support beneficial insects, and practice crop rotation in our growing fields.

Certification Programs

Programs like FSC and SFI certify that forest products come from responsibly managed sources. These certifications give consumers confidence that their purchases support sustainable practices.
