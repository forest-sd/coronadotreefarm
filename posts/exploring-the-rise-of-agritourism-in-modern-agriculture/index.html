The Rise of Agritourism in Modern Agriculture

Published: 2024 • By Coronado Tree Farm

Agritourism has emerged as a significant trend that connects urban populations with agricultural experiences while providing farmers with additional revenue streams.

What is Agritourism?

Agritourism encompasses farm visits, u-pick operations, farm stays, educational tours, corn mazes, and seasonal festivals. These activities give visitors hands-on experiences with farming while supporting local agriculture.

Benefits for Farms

  • Diversified income beyond crop sales
  • Direct-to-consumer marketing opportunities
  • Community connection and public education
  • Preservation of farmland through economic viability

Tree Farms and Agritourism

Tree farms are natural fits for agritourism. Christmas tree farms have long welcomed visitors, and year-round nurseries can offer classes, tours, and seasonal events that build community relationships and brand loyalty.
